You can come to our place:

By car:

From the Inner City of Budapest drive on Mainroad 7 /Road to Balaton/ and you will find us approx. 500 m ahead of the Statues Park, on the right side

By community transport:

Take bus 150 from Fehérvári út or Campona, and get off at stop Budatétény petrol station. Take long-distance coaches from Kelenföld Railway Station either to Százhalombatta (No. 710), Pusztazámor (No. 722) or Érd (No. 720), and get off at stop Budatétény petrol station.
